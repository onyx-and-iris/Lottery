# Lottery

![App Image](./img/lottery.png)

Generate numbers for the following lottery types:

#### UK Lotto

6 balls of 1-59

#### EuroMillions

5 balls of 1-50 and 2 lucky stars of 1-12

#### Set For Life

5 balls of 1-47 and 1 life ball of 1-10

#### Thunderball

5 balls of 1-39 and 1 thunderball of 1-14
